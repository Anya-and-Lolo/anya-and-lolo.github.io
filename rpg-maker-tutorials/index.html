Common Mistakes (So You Don’t Suffer)

These bite almost everyone at least once.
1
Parallel events doing too much

If something is Parallel, it should be tiny and gated (switch, timer, “only when changed” checks).

2
No backups or test projects

Always sandbox first! Testing in your main game is how you get “mystery bugs” that take days to resolve.

3
Hard-coding everything

Put repeatable logic into common events. Your future self will thank you!
