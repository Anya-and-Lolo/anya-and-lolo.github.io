WOLF RPG Editor FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions about downloading and using WOLF RPG Editor (Woditor).
Is WOLF RPG Editor free?
Yes! WOLF RPG Editor (Woditor) is completely free to download and use. You can even release games made with it, including commercial ones, though it is best to check the official terms for the current conditions and credit requirements.
Is WOLF RPG Editor available in English?
Although the original editor is Japanese, a complete English translation (en-US Woditor) is available, and our Complete English guide walks you through it.
What can you make with it?
It is highly flexible, so people build everything from classic turn-based RPGs to puzzle and horror games. Famous freeware titles like Ib, The Witch's House and Mad Father were made in WOLF RPG Editor.
Is it safe to download?
WOLF RPG Editor is a long-established and trusted engine. To stay safe, download it from the official ModDB page↗ or the IndieDB page↗, rather than random websites and re-uploads.
What are the system requirements?
WOLF RPG Editor runs on Windows. On macOS or Linux you can usually run it (and games made with it) through a compatibility layer such as Wine.
WOLF RPG Editor vs RPG Maker: which should I use?
RPG Maker is a paid, beginner-friendly engine with a huge plugin ecosystem. WOLF RPG Editor is free, with deeper customization but a steeper learning curve. If you want maximum control at no cost, Woditor is a great pick, and the Complete English guide helps you get started.
